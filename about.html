Little bit about me

 

Starting from my young age I was always passionate about electronics and computers. And because of that, my choice of High School choice was to go for a Computer Technician diploma, at Technical School Slavonski Brod, Croatia.

During my time in High School, being interested in every aspect of technology, I was very active in the field of electronic and computer engineering. That passion led to some of my personal, and school projects like "People Visit Counter" for the library. That project was very successful and still runs and count visit up to this date. 

Being successful student ,I have traveled to a lot of Science Fairs and be part of multiple exchange project where I had an opportunity to truly express myself and show my full potential to the world.

My projects and knowledge in the field granted me some of my awards, such as a Gold Medal for my Personal Project "IoT Development Board," and I am very proud for each every one of them. 

Currently I am studying Software Engineering at Nottingham Trent University, United Kingdom.

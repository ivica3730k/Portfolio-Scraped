Cattle Data Tool Project

During first term at the University, we were given a group project to do called "Cattle Data Tool."

Aim of the project was to develop an tracking aplication for the cattle that has tracking devices attached to them, and from that data later determine physical health of the Cow/Bull...That piece of sotware that we have developed is a part of experimental work with sensors and livestock, and is a part of a bigger project.

I was in charge of Data part of the project, creating database, importing files and sorting them all togather. For database I have used SQLite 3 database. Programing language for whole solution is Python.

My group and I were awarded with the highest grade for our solution.

Code is open source and you can find it here.

 

Universal IoT Development Board

One of my bigest project was developing of my own product called: "Universal IoT Development board."

At that time, board consisted of all electronic parts in one simple package, that was easy to use, in order to allow any user, even without any programming experience to quickly got their own IoT thing up in the cloud.

I personaly made all Electronic plans, manufactured board files, ordered blank board from China and populated it with neccesary compoments with my own soldering skills. All that at my own little workshop back in Croatia.

At that time, board was based on ESP8266 chip, and it included:

  • SMPS Power Supply
  • 16 Digital IO Ports
  • 6 ADC Ports
  • 5 DAC Outputs
  • RTC Clock
  • Oprional HC-05 Bluetooth module
  • Optional DHT22 Temperature and Humidity Sensor

...all packed in small form factor of 100*60mm.

That board was probably my bigest project I have completed so far. All my knowledge and skills were recognized. So, board granted me multiple awards in the field,like:

  1. Award for best Final Dissertation on topic "Universal IoT development board", Slavonski Brod,Croatia.May 2018.
  2. 1st place for "Universal IoT Development board" at regional Croatian contest "Mladi @ Inovacije 2018"
  3. 2nd place for "Universal IoT Development board" at "Science Fair Ivanic grad 2018"
  4. 3rd place for "Universal IoT Development board" at Inter-regional contest at University of Zagreb, Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing,2018

Currently I am designing better version of it and planning to make it on bigger scale.

Arduino RC Car

Back when I was at my high school in Croatia, I have designed simple RC Controled Vehicle called simply "Arduino RC Car." I created it in order to compite at the croatian science fair "Science Fair Ivanic Grad, 2017." 

Car consisted of 4WD electric system and was fully controlable over app on any Android device. Range was decent and battery life was good enough to last trough all 2 days of constant use.

The car was quite popular, bringing attention to young kids at the age of elementary school, probably inspiring them to be a "Techonolgy" persons one day. 

That project granted me 3rd place, bronze medal for that contest in that year.

Robo Challenge

During my high school in second grade I was part of “Robo Challenge” project that was held in my school back in Croatia.

Aim of the project was to introduce world of electronic and applied Software Engineering to the young students like me at that time. Project was separated in two main parts. First part was to assemble a robotic vehicle using appropriate tools, schematics and tools. Second part was to program that vehicle In order for it to do certain tasks like avoid an obstacle, follow line on floor, or to enable remote control.

My colleagues and I were very successful at that project and we have completed out tasks, and additional ones in time span of 2 weeks.

This project is one of my favourites because at that time, I was only 15 years old, and this was my first big step into the world of programming.
